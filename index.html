Puspa Mitta Sungha

Puspa Mitta Sungha was a soul of quiet strength and luminous heart, known for uplifting those around her with wisdom, kindness, and unwavering courage. Born from humble roots, she blossomed into a woman whose compassion touched many lives.

She believed that every being carries a spark of beauty and every moment holds meaning. Her journey was one of seeking truth, cherishing simplicity, and walking gently with others through both joy and hardship. Puspa’s presence was like a warm light in the darkness—steady, assuring, and true.
